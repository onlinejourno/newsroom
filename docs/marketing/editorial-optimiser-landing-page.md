# OnlineJourno Story Optimisation Platform
**From a Journalist, for Journalists**

Optimise every story across every discovery surface — Google Discover, Search & News, AI Overviews, AI assistants, and YouTube. Free, open-source, self-hosted. Point it at your newsroom's site and go.

**[ Coming soon · Follow on GitHub ]**

## Easy newsroom tech for journalists

Vendor-neutral, no lock-in. Download it, configure your newsroom in one file, and run it — no platform account required. Every score explains the *why* and the *how to fix*, so it teaches as it audits.

## What it does

- **Per-story audit** — SEO & E-E-A-T graded against Google's Search Quality Rater Guidelines.
- **Schema.org, fixed for you** — detects missing or wrong structured data and hands you the corrected JSON-LD (NewsArticle, VideoObject, and more).
- **Surface readiness** — scores each story for Discover, Search, News, AI Overviews, ChatGPT / Perplexity / Gemini, and YouTube — and you can add your own surfaces.
- **Trends & signals** — beat-scoped trending topics, enriched with Wikidata entities, with momentum and trajectory charts.
- **Core Web Vitals** — discovery dies on a slow page.
- **Prioritised fixes** — ranked by impact and effort, in plain language.

## How it works

1. **Download** from GitHub.
2. **Configure** your newsroom — sources, surfaces, beats, market — in one `newsroom.yaml`.
3. **Audit & optimise** — paste a URL or pull from your sitemap; get the report and the fixes.

## Two tiers — you choose

- **Free & keyless** — the full deterministic core runs with no API key, no cost.
- **Add your own AI** — plug in *any* LLM (Claude, GPT, Gemini, or local / Ollama) to unlock the 8 BBC-Smartocto user needs, AI-written schema suggestions, and an agentic SEO/GEO assistant. Your model, your key, your call.

## Why it's different

- **By a journalist, for journalists** — built around editorial judgement, not vanity metrics.
- **Vendor-neutral & open-source** — Apache-2.0; nothing hardcoded to any one outlet.
- **It teaches** — every finding shows the reasoning, so your desk learns.
