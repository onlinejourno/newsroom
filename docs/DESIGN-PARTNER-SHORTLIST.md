# Design Partner Shortlist (Stub)

To be filled Fri Jun 5.

## Selection criteria

A design partner must be:

1. A mid-size Indian publisher with a recognisable markets / regulatory desk.
2. Reachable through a warm intro (founder's existing network).
3. Willing to share one morning of shoulder-surfing with the editor (3–4 hrs).
4. Willing to use the brief for 8 weeks free, with an open conversation about a paid contract at Wk 12.
5. Not a direct competitor of OnlineJournalism.in's own coverage.

## Candidate template

For each:

- Publication name, beat focus, journalist headcount, current source-monitoring practice.
- Warm contact: name, role, relationship to founder.
- What they will get (free 8-week pilot, named in case study with consent, direct line to founder).
- What founder needs (one morning observation, 14 days of brief feedback, willingness to discuss paid contract Wk 12).
- Outreach message — one personalised paragraph.
