# Newsroom docs

> The agentic newsroom workspace — watch the public record, contextualise it
> against your own archive, and get ranked beat briefs.

## What it is

Newsroom is the full OnlineJourno workspace you sign into. It monitors the
sources that matter to your outlet, scores and tags each story against
established journalism scholarship, and surfaces what needs a reporter's
attention — before, during, and after publication.

## Who it's for

Working reporters and desk editors who can't watch every source by hand and want
the intelligence that usually pools at the top of the newsroom.

## How to use it

1. **See the demo.** [Open the read-only demo](https://app.onlinejourno.com/en/showcase)
   — real surfaces, sample data, no sign-in.
2. **Sign in / join** to work on your own tenant.
3. **Calendar / Plan** — start here: what's coming, what to commit to.
4. **Signals & beats** — ranked briefs from the monitored record.
5. **Grade a story** — pre-publish fair-chance cue; post-publish diagnostic.

## The lenses

Every score traces to a tested lens (PEJ framing, Deuze's typology, and merit
vs reach) — not vibes. Each surface explains the *why*, not just the verdict.

## Self-host

Fair-source (FSL). See [Self-hosting](/docs/self-hosting/) — bring your own
sources and signals via per-tenant config.
