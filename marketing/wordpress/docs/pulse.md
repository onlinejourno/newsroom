# Pulse docs

> A curated digest of Indian journalism that deserves more readers than it got.

## What it is

Pulse is a public, human-curated digest that surfaces high-merit, low-reach
stories from across Indian journalism — good work that quality outran reach.
It dogfoods the same merit-vs-reach thinking behind the rest of the suite, on
free, open sources.

## Who it's for

Readers who want the signal, and editors watching what travels and what doesn't.

## How to use it

1. Read the latest at [onlinejourno.com/in](https://onlinejourno.com/in/).
2. Each entry links the original and says, briefly, why it's worth your time.

## How stories are chosen

High editorial merit paired with low prominence — surfaced by the suite's
scoring, then chosen by a human. It is a showcase, not an aggregator: quality
over volume.
