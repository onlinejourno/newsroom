# Galley docs

> A graded report for a single story — like GTmetrix, but for journalism.

## What it is

Paste a story URL and Galley grades it across the surfaces that decide whether it
travels — Google Discover, Google News, and Search — then tells you where it's
strong, where it's losing readers, and the single highest-value fix.

## Who it's for

Reporters and SEO-minded editors who want a fast, honest read on one piece
before or after it publishes.

## How to use it

1. Open [galley.onlinejourno.com](https://galley.onlinejourno.com/).
2. Paste the story URL.
3. Read the grade and the per-surface breakdown.
4. Act on **the top fix** — the biggest *actionable* gap (Galley skips
   "no data" signals and points you at what you can actually change).

## Reading the report

Each channel (Discover / News / Search) shows a score, the signals behind it,
and a plain-language note. The grade is the weighted composite. The **top fix**
is the one change that moves the needle most.
