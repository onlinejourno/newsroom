# Frontmatter docs

> Merit should travel. Audit a whole feed for the deserving work that isn't.

## What it is

Connect your outlet's RSS feed and Frontmatter audits every recent story for the
**merit–reach gap** — the pieces whose quality outruns their reach — and ranks
the ones your distribution is failing, each with the fix.

## Who it's for

Editors and audience teams who want to know which good stories are being left on
the table, across the whole publication, not one at a time.

## How to use it

1. Open [frontmatter.onlinejourno.com](https://frontmatter.onlinejourno.com/).
2. Paste your feed URL (e.g. `https://your-outlet.com/feed`).
3. Frontmatter scores each story for merit and reach and ranks the gaps.
4. Work down the list — each row names the gap and the fix.

Auditing a single story instead? Use [Galley](/docs/galley/).

## Free tier

The public instance caps repository audits per day (compute-bound, keyless). For
unlimited, self-host or run it inside [Newsroom](/docs/newsroom/).
