# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 22 June 2026.*

OnlineJourno is built privacy-first. We collect as little as possible, and we do not track individuals.

## Analytics — cookieless, no personal data

This site uses **[Umami](https://umami.is/)**, a privacy-respecting, open-source analytics tool that we self-host. It:

- sets **no cookies**;
- collects **no personal data** and does not fingerprint or identify visitors;
- honours your browser's **Do Not Track** setting;
- stores only aggregate, anonymous usage counts (page views, referrers, country) on our own server.

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party data brokers.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell or share data.
- We don't build profiles of visitors.
- We don't embed third-party tracking pixels or ad networks.

## The application

Newsrooms using the OnlineJourno application (app.onlinejourno.com) own their own data. Where a newsroom **self-hosts**, their data stays on their own infrastructure entirely. Account information (name, email) is used only to operate the service and is never sold or shared.

## Your choices & contact

You can browse this site without accepting cookies — there are none to accept. For any data question, including access to or deletion of an account, contact us at **hello@onlinejourno.com**.
