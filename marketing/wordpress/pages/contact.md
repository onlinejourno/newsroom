# Contact

We'd like to hear from you — whether you run a newsroom, want a demo, or want to contribute.

## Get in touch

- **Email:** hello@onlinejourno.com
- **Request a demo:** ask for time-bound access to the live platform.

## For newsrooms

OnlineJourno can be configured to your sources, beats and tools, and self-hosted on your own infrastructure. Tell us about your newsroom and we'll help you get started.

## Contribute

OnlineJourno is built to be extended by the community. If you're a developer or a journalist with ideas, get in touch.
